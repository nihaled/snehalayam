About us

Welcome to Snehalayam

Snehalayam is a scheme under the Snehalayam initiative that focuses on providing invaluable medical and educational aids for the benefit of vulnerable sections of society. The Snehalayam Welfare Association came into being in 1999 under the guidance of Dr. P Snehalayam to help the neediest groups of people. It has put together various services to achieve sustainable development for society. While striving to bring a positive change in the whole society, Snehalayam has mainly focused on the empowerment of weaker sections of the society including widows, orphans, and students for various welfare programs. It is also geared to building sustainable community development through various livelihood betterment programs like housing for the homeless, employment schemes and self-employment opportunities for the unemployed, better educational opportunities, etc.

Our Mission

We are Snehalayam, the school of the divergent kids who get isolated from society. We create an atmosphere of love and care for such kids. Your kids who possess an unalike nature are safe in our hands with the distinct and creative way of our approaches. We are a team of compassion to nurture the best in your autistic kids.

02

“Creating difference to the differently-abled”

Snehalayam is an initiative aiming to build a support system for the differently abled kids in trissur. It aims to serve as a bridge, integrating the differently abled students in mainstream schools.
